About us:


MEA Engineering College was established in 2002 and is situated at Perinthalmanna, Malappuram, Kerala. It is the first NAAC accredited self-financing college in the engineering stream and has been awarded B+ Grade by the same. NBA has also accredited the college with 3 years of accreditation for CSE & MECH.

Courses offered: Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, B.Tech, B.Arch

All the above courses are offered in MEA Engineering College.
